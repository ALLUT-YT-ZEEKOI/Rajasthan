The Rajasthan Tile Method™

performance first, then beauty

A four-part judgment system we built over four decades — so your tile is chosen for the life it has to survive, not just the showroom light it sits under.

01

Impact

How much weight, footfall and daily abuse the surface must absorb without chipping or cracking.

02

Exposure

Water, heat, sunlight, chemicals — the conditions the tile faces, and whether its body can take them.

03

Maintenance

How easy it is to keep clean over years — stain resistance, grout behaviour and real cleaning cycles.

04

Longevity

Whether it still looks intentional in ten years — or quietly ages into something you'll want to replace.
