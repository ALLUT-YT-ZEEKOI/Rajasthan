Our Heritage

A legacy born
from one decision.

Rajasthan Marbles was founded in 1984 with a single conviction: that the materials used to build a home deserve the same care and discernment as the vision behind it.

What began as a modest showroom in Ernakulam grew into one of Kerala's most trusted building material destinations — not through aggressive expansion, but through honest guidance and an uncompromising eye for quality.

Today, four experience centres serve thousands of homeowners, architects, contractors, and developers each year. The philosophy, however, remains unchanged: we help you decide with confidence.
