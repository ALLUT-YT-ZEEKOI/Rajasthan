Common questions

Before you visit.

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins are welcome. For large projects or trade enquiries, calling ahead helps us assign the right advisor.

Can you deliver to my site?

Yes. We supply across Ernakulam and neighbouring districts. Delivery timelines depend on product availability.

Do you offer design advice?

Our advisors help you choose materials for your space — not full interior design, but honest guidance on what works.

What should I bring?

Floor plans, room dimensions, and photos of your space help us recommend faster. Samples of existing finishes are useful too.
